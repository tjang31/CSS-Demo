Box Model

    First, open in browser and take a look at DOM.
    Next, the text is in the content and that includes all text, images, and videos.
    Padding is the space between text and the gray color. When using background-color, increasing padding will give it more space at all angles.
    The border is an outline of the content. For example, a picture frame has a border.
    You can change a border's: color, style, and width.
    The margin is the side of the page from the border. It is the most outer part of the content.
    Keep in mind that margin, border, and padding are invisble to the naked eye.
    Last but not least, each element contains a border!
